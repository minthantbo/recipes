Shan Noodles

Return to Home

Shan Noodles—one of the most popular dishes in Myanmar—is a perfect blend of rice noodles, a tangy tomato-based sauce, and either chicken or pork, topped with fresh herbs and vegetables. It's not only filling but relatively light, with lots of nutrients coming from the fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Rice Noodles
  • Shredded chicken: or ground pork
  • Tomatoes, garlic, onions
  • Spices: including turmeric, paprika, and chili powder
  • Pickled mustard greens: for a sour contrast
  • Fresh herbs: like cilantro and spring onions

Steps

  1. Place the chicken carcass, garlic, peppercorns and water in a large saucepan.
  2. Bring to a boil then reduce the heat. Allow to simmer for 1 to 2 hours.
  3. Prepare the chicken sauce.
  4. Heat the oil in a small saucepan.
  5. Add the garlic, stir through until fragrant but without catching colour.
  6. Add chilli powder, diced chicken, tomatoes and soy sauce.
  7. Simmer gently for 10 minutes until the tomatoes have softened and any liquid has disappeared.
  8. Prepare the garnishes and cook the noodles.
  9. Rinse the noodles in plenty of cold water to keep them from clumping together once cooked.
  10. Put the noodles in a bowl, a generous spoonful of chicken, ladle over with soup including the spinach.
